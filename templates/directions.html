Founders Great Hall
You can't miss Founders. It is smack-dab in the heart of campus. From the Stokes Bus Stop, just walk towards Magill and look left to where all of the sidewalks converge. From the South Parking Lot head upcampus towards Stokes. Follow the side-walks and you'll find yourself on Founders Green! Founders is the large tan/white building with the nice rocking chairs.
Parking
If you don't want to take the Blue Bus or the Swat Van, parking is also available in the South Lot and by Stokes. The South Lot is probably your best bet for availability.
